Welcome to Pompei

Welcome to Pompei, a city steeped in history and brimming with culture. Located in the Campania region of southern Italy, Pompei is renowned worldwide for its archaeological treasures, including the ancient ruins of Pompeii, which offer a captivating glimpse into life during the Roman Empire.

Explore History

As you wander through the streets of Pompei, you'll find yourself transported back in time to the bustling city that once thrived here. From the remarkably preserved buildings and artifacts to the haunting plaster casts of the city's inhabitants, every corner tells a story of the past, offering a unique opportunity to explore one of the world's most famous archaeological sites.

Culinary Delights

Beyond its archaeological wonders, Pompei is also a vibrant modern city, where tradition and innovation coexist harmoniously. Visitors can sample the region's renowned cuisine, which draws on fresh, locally sourced ingredients and centuries-old recipes. From mouthwatering pizzas and pastas to delectable seafood dishes, Pompei's culinary scene is sure to delight even the most discerning palate.

Vibrant Cultures

In addition to its historical and gastronomic offerings, Pompei boasts a rich cultural heritage, with a calendar packed full of events and festivals celebrating everything from music and art to local traditions and folklore. Whether you're strolling through a lively street market, attending a classical concert in a historic theater, or taking part in a traditional religious procession, you'll find plenty of opportunities to immerse yourself in Pompei's vibrant cultural scene.

Outdoor Adventures

For those seeking outdoor adventure, Pompei is ideally situated near the rugged beauty of the Amalfi Coast and the majestic slopes of Mount Vesuvius. Whether you're hiking along scenic coastal trails, exploring hidden coves and beaches, or marveling at panoramic views from the summit of an active volcano, Pompei offers endless opportunities for exploration and discovery amidst breathtaking natural landscapes.

Chamber Information

Pompei's Chamber of Commerce serves as a vital resource for businesses, both local and international, seeking to establish or expand their presence in the region. With a dedicated team of professionals, the Chamber provides a wide range of services and support to help businesses thrive in Pompei's dynamic economic landscape. From assisting with business registration and licensing to providing market research and networking opportunities, the Chamber is committed to fostering a vibrant business community and driving economic growth in Pompei. Additionally, the Chamber plays a key role in promoting tourism and attracting investment to the region, helping to showcase Pompei's unique assets and opportunities to a global audience. Whether you're a new entrepreneur looking to launch a startup or an established company seeking to expand into new markets, the Chamber of Commerce is here to support you every step of the way. With its wealth of resources, expertise, and connections, the Chamber is your trusted partner for success in Pompei's thriving business ecosystem.
